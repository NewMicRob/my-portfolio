About Me

My name is Michael, most people call me Mike. I am still enrolled in the Computer Programming program at Humber Polytechnica. I was born in Brampton and moved to Caledon when I was 5. I live in Caledon which is just to the North of Vaughan. I am interested in gaming and tech in general. I spend most of my time reading books/ graphic novels (Between Two Fires and Solo Leveling), playing video games (currently playing Silent Hill 2 Remake), and watching TV shows/movies (Landman, and Alien Romulus). I have a black and white Boston terrier named Anna who is 7 years old. I went to Japan last month with my longtime girlfriend and her friends. We went for 10 days and had some amazing food/drinks(7-11 smoothies, shoyu ramen), visited some nice shrines/temples (Fushimi Inari Taisha, Todai-ji Temple, etc.) and went to 4 cities(Tokyo, Kyoto, Nara, Osaka). It was a great trip, the only downside was the 14 hr flight.
